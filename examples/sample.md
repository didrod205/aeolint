# What Is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so
that AI answer engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews and others —
can find, quote and cite it. Where classic SEO optimizes for a ranked list of
blue links, AEO optimizes for being **the answer**.

## How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO and AEO overlap, but they optimize for different surfaces:

- **SEO** wants you to rank in a list of results a human scrolls through.
- **AEO** wants an AI to extract a concise, correct answer from your page and
  cite you as the source.

In practice that means clear question-style headings, a direct answer near the
top, structured data (schema.org), and self-contained, factual paragraphs.

## What should I actually do?

1. Lead each section with a short, direct answer (one or two sentences).
2. Phrase headings as the questions people ask.
3. Add `FAQPage` and `Article` JSON-LD.
4. Keep paragraphs self-contained so a single quote still makes sense.
5. Make sure the page is crawlable (no accidental `noindex`).
